TMC - Clinical Assistant

Thompson Medical Centre was my summer 2023 employment. As a clinical assistant, I was in charge of bringing in patients and performing intake history in Accuro. I also prepared dermatology surgery trays, which included drawing up syringes of local anaesthetic and sterilizing tools with an autoclave. I had the opportunity to research and create patient information sheets about skin cancer and sun safety using Google Docs. Lastly, I performed medical clerical work, such as handling incoming and outgoing mail.

Skills Used

  • Working with individuals with varying physical and mental capabilities
  • Maintaining confidentiality
  • Working in a fast paced environment
  • Autoclave and stanitization of medical examination rooms
  • Drawing up syringes
  • Google Docs
  • Research
  • Clerical duties
