Biotron - An EMG Fabric Biosleeve

Biotron is a design team at the Univeristy of Waterloo where we make biomechatronic devices to aid specific individuals or to advance the accessibility of biomedical devices. I am a part of the EMG Fabric subteam, where we are creating a heart-monitoring and gesture recognition sleeve.
